Guérin, Gabriel
France

	Gabriel Fernand Charles Guérin was born 25 July 1892 in Morocco. He entered military service in October 1913 serving with the 28e Regiment d'Infantrie    In August 1916, he transferred as a student pilot and received his Brevet 10 December. He was assigned to Esc N.15 on 25 April 1917. Guérin lost little time getting noticed; he scored his first victory a month later, by the time he had scored his 4th victory he was a sergeant, and on 27 August he received the Medaille Militaire.  In December he was promoted to Sous Lieutenant.  On 27 February 1918, he was made a Chevalier de la Legion d'Honneur.  On 11 May, after his 22nd victory he was wounded in action and spent the next few weeks in hospital.  On 7 July, he took command of Esc Spa88, and was promoted to Lieutenant on the 13th.  Gabriel Guérin was killed in a flying accident at Mont l'Eveque on 1 August when his SPAD SVII spun into the ground shortly after take off. He was 26.
