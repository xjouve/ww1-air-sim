Chainat, Andre 
France

   Andre Julien Chainat was born 27 June 1892 at La Chapelle Saint Laurian (Indre). He entered military service with the Artillerie in October 1913, but transferred to the 2eme Groupe d'Aviation on 22 April 1914. He was assigned to Esc B4 20 July 1914.
  After being selected for pilot training, he earned his Brevet and was assigned to MS23 29 June 1915, then to MS38 on 1 November. On 17 January 1916 he transferred to Esc N3 - Les Cigognes. He was wounded several times, and decorated on numerous occasions. After being wounded again on September 7, 1916, he transferred to the 3eme Groupe d'Aviation.
  Andre Chainat died 6 November 1961 in Cannes.
